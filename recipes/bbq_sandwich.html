Dustin's Pulled Pork Sandwich

Description

Slow cooked pulled pork, tangy sauce, and a pillowy bun. What more could you want?

Ingredients

  • BBQ Pulled Pork
  • Sweet and spicy BBQ sauce
  • A soft sandwich roll
  • Crinkle-cut fries

Steps

  1. Drive to Edgewater, Fl
  2. Locate Dustins BBQ
  3. Order that pulled pork sandwich combo
  4. Put fries on sandwich
  5. Apply BBQ sauce
  6. Apply sandwich directly to mouth
