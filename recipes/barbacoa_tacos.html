Barbacoa Tacos

Description

Crispy tacos loaded with juicy barbacoa, spicy salsa, and overpriced guacamole. The perfect lunch break meal.

Ingredients

  • Barbacoa
  • Sauteed onions and peppers

  • Crispy tortilla shell
  • Guacamole
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Open the Chipotle app
  2. Order those tacos
  3. Arrive on location
  4. Pick up food and enjoy!
