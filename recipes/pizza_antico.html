Pizza Antico

Description

Fresh mozzarella, mini cup & char pepperoni & fresh basil. We named this pizza Antico which means antique because we use the same fresh mozzarella, mini cup & char pepperoni the first pizzerias used in NY since 1905

Ingredients

  • Fresh Mozarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Mini Cup and Char pepperoni
  • Ultra thin pizza crust

Steps

  1. Drive to Gainesville
  2. Call Piesanos
  3. Order that pizza
  4. Endure the wait
  5. Pick up the pizza
  6. Eat it in the car on the way home
